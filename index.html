T.O.A.S.T.

The Original Aristic Solution for Toast

Summary

T.O.A.S.T is your next breakfast solution. Unlike an ordinary toaster, this device will be able to take any average slice of bread and toast messages, the weather, or even your face onto the surface to make your breakfast experience more interesting. Beyond the novelty, we see great potential for the product. Wireless communication can be integrated such that your friends can send you toast messages, the weather for the day can be retrieved from online, and images from the internet can be processed to be toasted.
